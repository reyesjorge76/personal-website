Our Story

Piston & Chrome started as a dream—bringing together the best of two worlds: **classic cars, fast bikes, and unforgettable flavors**. We built a mobile food trailer to take our handcrafted tacos to every car show, biker meetup, and late-night street corner. We’re here to serve **bold flavors with an extra dose of horsepower.** 🚀
